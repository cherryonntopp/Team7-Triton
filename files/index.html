About My Topic

This website is a one-stop food resource hub for the UC San Diego community, built to make campus food support more accessible and easier to navigate. It combines Triton Food Pantry inventory information,food-related campus events in calendar form, and recipe ideas based on pantry items so students can quickly find what they need.By bringing these resources together, the site aims to support food security, encourage student engagement, and build a more connected campus community. The website is intended mainly for UC San Diego students, especially those seeking convenient, affordable, and practical food resources
